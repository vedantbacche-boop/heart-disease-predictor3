What is Heart Disease?

Heart disease refers to a range of conditions that affect the heart, including coronary artery disease, arrhythmias, and heart valve problems. It's a leading cause of death worldwide.

Maintaining a healthy lifestyle, regular exercise, and medical checkups help prevent heart disease.

About This Website

The model was trained using the Random Forest algorithm on a dataset of heart health indicators.
