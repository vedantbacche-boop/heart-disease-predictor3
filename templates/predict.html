💡 About This Project

This website uses a Machine Learning model 🤖 trained on medical data to predict whether a person might have heart disease 🫀.

Our goal is to make early detection easier and spread awareness so that people can take charge of their health 💪.

Note: This is not a medical diagnosis tool — it’s an educational project designed to demonstrate AI’s potential in healthcare 🌍.

❤️ Heart Disease Awareness

Every year, heart disease affects millions globally 🌎. But with awareness, prevention, and care, we can change that.

  • 🥗 Eat more fruits and vegetables.
  • 🚶‍♂️ Exercise for at least 30 minutes a day.
  • 🚭 Avoid smoking and limit alcohol intake.
  • 🧘 Manage stress with meditation or hobbies.
  • 💤 Get proper sleep and routine checkups.

Remember — a strong heart builds a stronger life ❤️.
